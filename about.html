about me

Hello! I’m a graphic designer born in San Juan, Puerto Rico currently living in Las Vegas, NV. I'm an illustration-based designer who likes to work in fun, colorful styles, and sometimes vintage-inspired design, mostly through illustration, 3D, and motion design.
At a young age I taught myself how to use Photoshop through old web and video tutorials and to this day I enjoy the challenge of learning new programs to help actualize my creativity. I specialize in anything to do with games, art, and music and you’ll never see me without a sketchbook at hand and a playlist ready to play.
